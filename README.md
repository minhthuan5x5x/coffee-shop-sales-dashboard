# ☕ Brew Coffee - Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
Built a Power BI dashboard to analyze sales performance for a coffee business with 3 store locations over a 6-month period. Used Power Query, data modeling, and DAX measures to track KPIs, product performance, and revenue trends from 149K+ sales transactions.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives
- Analyze sales performance across time, products, and store locations
- Build a clean and scalable data model using Star Schema
- Create an interactive dashboard for business monitoring and reporting
- Provide business insights and recommendations based on sales analysis

---

# 🛠️ Methodology

## 1️⃣ Data Preparation (Power Query)
- Imported raw CSV data
- Performed data cleaning, type conversion, and quality checks
- Created calculated columns:
  - Total Revenue
  - Hour
  - Day of Weekz
- Designed a Star Schema with Fact and Dimension tables

## 2️⃣ Data Modeling
- Created a Date Table for time intelligence analysis
- Established relationships between tables
- Structured the data model for easier reporting and analysis

## 3️⃣ Analysis & Visualization (Power BI)
- Created DAX measures for KPIs and sales analysis
- Built a professional 4-page interactive dashboard


---

# 📊 Dashboard Structure

## Page 1 – Overview
- Total Revenue & Orders
- Month-over-Month (MoM) Growth
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Store & Product Category
## Page 2 – Time Intelligence
- Hourly Sales Performance
- Peak Sales Hour Identification
- Daily & Weekly Revenue Analysis
- Revenue Heatmap Visualization

## Page 3 – Product Performance
- Top & Bottom Selling Products
- Category Contribution
- Quantity & Pricing Analysis

## Page 4 – Store Performance
- Store Revenue Comparison
- Store-Level KPI Benchmarking
- Product Mix by Store
- Revenue & Order Distribution


---

# 🔍 Key Insights
- Coffee and Tea products contributed nearly 67% of total revenue, making them the primary revenue drivers across all stores.
- Sales activity peaked between 8:00 AM and 11:00 AM, indicating strong morning demand patterns.
- Monthly revenue demonstrated a consistent upward trend from March to June, reflecting improving sales performance over time.
- All three store locations showed relatively balanced revenue distribution, with Hell’s Kitchen slightly leading overall sales performance.

---

# 💡 Recommendations
- Increase upselling efforts for Flavours and Bakery products
- Optimize staff scheduling during peak morning hours
- Introduce seasonal summer beverage options
- Focus marketing efforts on premium products such as Coffee Beans and Loose Tea
- Monitor product performance regularly to identify declining items and adjust inventory planning accordingly.
